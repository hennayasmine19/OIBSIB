                <div class="bio">
                    <div>
                        <h4>Early Life</h4>
                        <p>Matthew Perry was born on August 19, 1969, in Williamstown, Massachusetts.Raised in Ottawa, Canada, he developed an early interest in acting and comedy.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Acting Career</h4>
                        <p>Matthew Perry's acting career spans decades, with memorable roles that have captivated audiences around the world.Notable roles include Chandler Bing in 'Friends,' Nicholas 'Oz' Oseransky in 'The Whole Nine Yards,' and Matt Albie in 'Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Philanthropy</h4>
                       <p>Matthew Perry is not only a talented actor but also a philanthropist dedicated to making a positive impact.He actively supports charities focused on addiction recovery and mental health, contributing to causes that resonate with him.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Death</h4>
                        <p>Matthew Perry died at the age of 54 from an apparent drowning, according to TMZ. The publication reported that the Friends actor was found dead in a hot tub at his home in Los Angeles, California, on Saturday, October 28, 2023.</p>
                    </div>
                    <div>
                        <h4>Legacy</h4>
                        <p>Matthew Perry's legacy extends beyond the screen, leaving an incredible mark on the entertainment industry.His contributions to comedy and his advocacy work serve as an inspiration to aspiring actors and individuals facing personal challenges.</p>
